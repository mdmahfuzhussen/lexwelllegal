Who We Are

A firm founded
on principle.

LAWLEX was established in 1999 by two partners who believed the legal profession had lost sight of the person behind every case. Their conviction was simple: every client deserves a lawyer who listens first and fights hard second.

Today we are a team of 50 attorneys across 12 practice areas. Our size gives us the depth of a large firm and the attention of a boutique — and we have never lost sight of what we were built on.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Integrity

We say what we mean and do what we say. Honest counsel — even when the news is difficult — is the foundation of every client relationship we build.

Client First

Your outcome is our measure of success. We listen carefully, advise clearly, and adapt our strategy to your specific circumstances — not a template.

Precision

Legal work lives or dies on the details. Our attorneys are thorough, methodical, and leave nothing to chance — from the first document to the final argument.

Justice

We believe the law exists to protect people. We take that seriously in every case, regardless of complexity or scale, and we fight until the work is done right.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

1999

The Founding

Partners James Whitmore and Eleanor Reid open LAWLEX with a team of four, specialising in family and civil law from a single office in London.

2005

Corporate Division Launched

Growing demand from business clients prompted the launch of a dedicated corporate and commercial practice, doubling headcount within two years.

2011

National Recognition

LAWLEX is named Regional Law Firm of the Year for the first time, recognising consistent client outcomes across criminal, civil, and corporate practice.

2018

International Expansion

We open our second office and establish international partnerships to support clients with cross-border business, immigration, and tax matters.

2024

50 Attorneys Strong

LAWLEX reaches 50 attorneys across 12 practice areas — and remains committed to the same values that guided those first four desks in 1999.
